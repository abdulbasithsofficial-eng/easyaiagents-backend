FAQ

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers

Do I need coding skills to build an agent? ▾
Absolutely not. EasyAIAgents is built for non-technical entrepreneurs. If you can write an email, you can build an AI agent. Our 3-step wizard handles everything — just pick a template, paste your business info, and click deploy.
How does the OTP login work? ▾
When you sign up or log in, we send a 6-digit one-time password to your email address. Enter it in the app to verify your identity. It expires in 10 minutes and is completely secure — we never store your password.
Can I connect to WhatsApp? ▾
Yes! Professional and Agency plans include WhatsApp Business API integration. Connect your WhatsApp number and your agent will automatically handle incoming messages, qualify leads, and respond to customers 24/7.
What happens after my free trial? ▾
After your 14-day trial, you'll be prompted to choose a plan. Your agents and data are saved. If you don't upgrade, your agents are paused (not deleted) so you can continue anytime without losing your setup.
Can I train the agent in Urdu or Arabic? ▾
Yes! You can train your agent with content in any language and set it to respond in Urdu, Arabic, Hindi, English, or 50+ other languages. Your agent will automatically detect the customer's language and respond accordingly.
Is my data secure? ▾
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We're GDPR compliant and never sell your data to third parties. Agency plan customers get dedicated isolated infrastructure.
